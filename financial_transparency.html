Why Financial Openness Matters

Transparent finances are the bedrock of a healthy community. They build trust, drive economic stability, and empower citizens.

What Excellent Transparency Looks Like

Leading government finance organizations set clear benchmarks for ideal financial reporting. Here are the key pillars.

Dual Annual Reports

Publish a highly detailed, audited report (ACFR) for experts and a simple, visual summary (PAFR) for the public.

A Clear, Public Budget

Release a well-organized annual budget that clearly explains the city's spending priorities and funding sources for the upcoming year.

Easy Digital Access

Make all documents easy to find online and provide interactive tools that allow citizens to explore the data themselves.

Limits of This Analysis

This analysis confirms that Ridgefield does an excellent job of openly sharing its financial information according to established accounting rules.
